Factory Performance & Production Improvement

Manufacturing performance is rarely limited by machinery alone. Hidden inefficiencies in planning, flow, and process often cost far more than they appear to.

What a Factory Performance Audit Covers

A structured, on-the-ground review of how a facility actually operates — looking beyond the headline output numbers to find where time, capacity, and margin are being lost.

We work with manufacturing businesses to examine production planning and flow, machine utilisation, downtime patterns, material movement, inventory control, quality control processes, maintenance practices, and factory layout — building a clear picture of where the constraints actually sit.

Why Production Plants Underperform

In most facilities we look at, the gap between current and possible output isn't caused by one dramatic failure — it's a build-up of smaller issues: unclear planning handoffs, machines running below rated capacity, quality issues caught too late, and reporting that doesn't reach the people who could act on it.

Identifying which of these actually matter, in order of impact, is the point of the audit.
