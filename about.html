About Us

A house built on relationships

Registered as a partnership firm in Lahore, trading and advising since 2024.

JABRAN & CO began operating in 2024, and was formally registered as a partnership firm in Lahore in May 2026 under the Partnership Act, 1932 — a step that brought years of hands-on trading and advisory work under a single, compliant corporate structure built for sustainable growth.

Registered as a partnership firm in Lahore under the Partnership Act, 1932, the firm's scope spans consulting, general trading, import-export, wholesale & retail, sourcing and procurement, logistics coordination, and dealings with government and semi-government entities.
