Our website chat widget and our internal assistants are powered by AI. Please do not share passwords, payment-card details or sensitive personal information in the chat. AI replies can be wrong — nothing the chat says is a binding offer or professional advice, and every engagement is scoped and confirmed by a human.

9. Retention

  • Enquiries that do not become engagements: normally up to 24 months.
  • Client and financial records: retained for as long as required by Pakistani tax and corporate law, and for the period any claim could arise.
  • Portal accounts: for the life of the relationship, then closed on request.
  • Chat transcripts and audit logs: retained for security, quality and record-keeping, then deleted on a rolling basis.

10. Security

We take security seriously and design for it rather than bolt it on:

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS); data is encrypted at rest by our infrastructure providers.
  • Access is enforced in the database itself through row-level security, so a person can only ever read what their role permits — clients see only their own organisation.
  • Passwords are stored only as salted hashes by our authentication provider. We never see them.
  • Administrative actions and sensitive access are written to an audit log.
  • Sessions can be revoked across every device, and staff sessions carry a traceability watermark.

No system is perfectly secure. If a breach affects you, we will act promptly and tell you where we are required, or where it is right, to do so.
